11 JUN 2024 - Build a NAS

Parts:

  • FriendlyELEC CM3588 NAS Kit - $184
  • SSD Cards

Resources:

  • Open Media Vault

Having two kids eats up A LOT, and I mean A LOT of storage on your phone. I have a 128GB iPhone and I've had to offload photos and videos a few times. And while it's easy enough to just upload them to Google Photos, I'm not a fan of the recurring costs.

Enter the NAS!

I'm using the FriendlyELEC CM3588 NAS Kit. It's a 4-bay NAS with a 2.5" SATA drive bay. It has a 2.5Gbps ethernet port, 2 USB 3.0 ports, and a PCIe 2.0 x2 slot.

Unfortunately, I'm not actually starting this project, yet. I'm just creating a blog post for my other ongoing project... This blog!

I will post updates when I'm able to begin the NAS project.
